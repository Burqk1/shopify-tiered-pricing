# Free Gifts

Offer free gifts with purchase to increase order value, clear inventory, and delight your customers. Free gifts are a powerful incentive that can boost conversions and encourage larger orders.

{% hint style="info" %}
**Plan Required:** GROWTH or PROFESSIONAL
{% endhint %}

## How Free Gifts Work

When customers meet specified conditions (spend amount, buy specific products, or reach quantity thresholds), a free gift is automatically added to their cart at $0.

```
┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
│  🎁 FREE GIFT UNLOCKED!                         │
│                                                 │
│  [Sample Size Moisturizer]         FREE ($15)   │
│  Added automatically to your cart               │
└─────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
```

## Gift Trigger Types

### Spend Threshold

Gift added when cart reaches amount:
- "Spend $100, get a free tote bag"
- "Orders over $75 include a free sample set"

### Product Purchase

Gift added when specific products are bought:
- "Buy any perfume, get free travel atomizer"
- "Purchase this skincare set, receive bonus serum"

### Quantity Threshold

Gift added when buying X items:
- "Buy 3 items, get a free item"
- "Purchase 5 candles, receive free matches"

### Collection Purchase

Gift for buying from specific collection:
- "Buy from Holiday Collection, get free ornament"
- "Shop Summer Sale, receive beach tote"

### Specific Product

Gift tied to one product purchase:
- "Buy this coffee maker, get free coffee beans"
- "Purchase starter kit, receive bonus accessories"

## Creating a Free Gift Offer

### Step 1: Navigate to Gifts

1. Go to **Free Gifts** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Gift Offer**

### Step 2: Configure Trigger

**Trigger Type**: Select from options above

**Trigger Value**:
- For spend: Enter amount (e.g., $100)
- For product: Select product(s)
- For quantity: Enter number
- For collection: Select collection

### Step 3: Select Gift Product

1. Click **Select Gift Product**
2. Search your inventory
3. Choose the gift item
4. If variants exist, select specific variant or let customer choose

**Gift Settings:**
- Quantity: How many gifts to add (default: 1)
- Max per order: Limit gifts per order
- Inventory deduction: Track gift inventory or not

### Step 4: Display Settings

**Gift Announcement:**
- Show popup when gift unlocked
- Display in cart drawer
- Add to cart page messaging

**Progress Indicator:**
- Show how close customer is to gift
- "Spend $25 more to get your free gift!"

### Step 5: Conditions (Optional)

Add additional requirements:
- Customer tags (VIP only)
- First-time customers only
- Specific date range
- Geographic restrictions

### Step 6: Activate

1. Review configuration
2. Click **Create Gift Offer**
3. Toggle to **Active**

## Multi-Tier Gift System

Create escalating gift rewards:

```
Tier 1: Spend $50  → Free sample (value $5)
Tier 2: Spend $100 → Free full-size product (value $25)
Tier 3: Spend $200 → Free premium gift set (value $75)
```

**Benefits:**
- Encourages higher spending
- Multiple motivation points
- Clear value progression

**Setup:**
1. Create multiple gift offers
2. Set different thresholds
3. Assign gifts to each tier
4. Enable "Show next tier" messaging

## Gift Settings

### Inventory Management

**Track Gift Inventory:**
- ON: Gift deducted from inventory, limited availability
- OFF: Unlimited gifts (for digital or high-stock items)

**Low Stock Behavior:**
- Continue offer without gift
- Pause offer automatically
- Switch to alternate gift

### Customer Limits

**Per Customer:**
- Unlimited
- One per order
- One per customer (lifetime)
- One per month

**Per Order:**
- Maximum gifts per order
- Prevents gaming the system

### Removal Rules

What happens if customer removes items and no longer qualifies:
- Auto-remove gift from cart
- Keep gift but show warning
- Require minimum for checkout

## Best Practices

### 1. Choose Valuable Gifts

- Perceived value matters more than cost
- Show original price crossed out
- "Gift valued at $XX"

### 2. Keep Gift Relevant

- Match gift to purchase
- Complement main products
- Introduce new products as samples

### 3. Use for Inventory Management

- Gift slow-moving inventory
- Sample new products
- Clear seasonal items

### 4. Set Appropriate Thresholds

| Your AOV | Gift Threshold | Gift Value |
|----------|---------------|------------|
| $50 | $65-75 | $10-15 |
| $75 | $100-120 | $15-25 |
| $100 | $130-150 | $25-40 |

### 5. Create Urgency

- "While supplies last"
- Limited-time offers
- Countdown timers

### 6. Promote the Offer

- Homepage banner
- Email campaigns
- Social media
- Product page callouts

## Troubleshooting

### Gift not adding to cart

1. Verify customer meets trigger conditions
2. Check gift product is in stock
3. Confirm customer hasn't reached limit
4. Verify offer is "Active"

### Gift adding incorrectly

1. Check trigger type and value
2. Verify no duplicate offers
3. Review condition settings
4. Check product/collection targeting

### Customer complaints about gift

1. Ensure gift inventory is available
2. Verify correct variant is adding
3. Check gift product settings in Shopify
4. Review removal rules if items were removed
