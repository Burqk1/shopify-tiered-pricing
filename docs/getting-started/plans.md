## How Billing Works

### Shopify Billing

All payments are processed through Shopify's billing system:

- Charges appear on your Shopify invoice
- 30-day billing cycle
- No separate payment method needed
- Cancel anytime from Settings

### Free Trial

- New users get a **7-day free trial** of GROWTH features
- No credit card required during trial
- Automatically downgrades to FREE if not upgraded

### Upgrading

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing & Plans**
2. Click **Upgrade** on your desired plan
3. Approve the charge in Shopify
4. Features unlock immediately

### Downgrading

1. Go to **Settings** > **Billing & Plans**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Confirm the cancellation
4. Access continues until current billing period ends
5. Downgrades to FREE plan

{% hint style="warning" %}
When downgrading, extra rules beyond FREE limit will be paused, not deleted. You can reactivate them if you upgrade again.
{% endhint %}

## Locked Features

When you see a 🔒 icon next to a feature:

1. **In Navigation**: The feature requires a higher plan
2. **Clicking**: Takes you to the upgrade page
3. **Visual**: Appears faded/dimmed

This helps you discover all available features while clearly showing what's included in your current plan.

## Choosing the Right Plan

### Choose FREE if:
- You're just starting out
- You only need one discount rule
- You want to test before committing

### Choose GROWTH if:
- You have multiple product categories
- You want BOGO or bundle deals
- You sell B2B/wholesale
- You need to A/B test pricing

### Choose PROFESSIONAL if:
- You sell internationally
- You want AI-powered optimization
- You need deep analytics
- You have a large product catalog

## FAQ

**Q: Can I switch plans anytime?**
A: Yes, upgrade or downgrade whenever you want.

**Q: Do I lose my data when downgrading?**
A: No, rules are paused not deleted. Upgrade again to reactivate.

**Q: Is there a yearly discount?**
A: Contact support for annual pricing options.

**Q: Can I get a custom plan?**
A: Enterprise plans available for high-volume stores. Contact support.
