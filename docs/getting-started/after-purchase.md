# After Purchase - What to Do Next

Congratulations on your purchase! This guide will walk you through exactly what to set up and where.

## Step 1: Open the App

1. Go to your **Shopify Admin** panel
2. Click **Apps** in the left sidebar
3. Click **Tiered Pricing Pro**

You'll see the main dashboard.

## Step 2: Choose Your Plan (if not already)

If you haven't selected a plan yet:

1. Go to **Settings** → **Billing**
2. Choose your plan:
   - **FREE**: 1 pricing rule, basic features
   - **GROWTH** ($19.99/mo): Unlimited rules + BOGO, Bundles, Timers, etc.
   - **PROFESSIONAL** ($49.99/mo): Everything + AI Pricing, Analytics, API

{% hint style="info" %}
Start with FREE to test, upgrade anytime from Settings → Billing.
{% endhint %}

## What Do You Want to Do?

### 🏷️ "I want to give bulk/volume discounts"

**Go to: Pricing Rules**

Example: "Buy 2 get 10% off, Buy 5 get 20% off"

1. Click **Pricing Rules** in sidebar
2. Click **Create Rule**
3. Set:
   - **Name**: "Volume Discount"
   - **Products**: Choose which products
   - **Tiers**: Add quantity-based discounts
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/pricing-rules/creating-rules.md)

---

### 🎁 "I want Buy One Get One (BOGO) offers"

**Go to: BOGO Offers** *(GROWTH plan)*

Example: "Buy 2 shirts, get 1 free"

1. Click **BOGO Offers** in sidebar
2. Click **Create BOGO**
3. Set:
   - **Type**: Buy X Get Y Free
   - **Buy Quantity**: 2
   - **Get Quantity**: 1
   - **Products**: Select products
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/bogo/README.md)

---

### 📦 "I want to sell product bundles"

**Go to: Bundles** *(GROWTH plan)*

Example: "Starter Kit - 3 products for $49.99"

1. Click **Bundles** in sidebar
2. Click **Create Bundle**
3. Set:
   - **Name**: "Starter Kit"
   - **Products**: Add products to bundle
   - **Discount**: Set bundle price or discount
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/bundles/README.md)

---

### 🚚 "I want a free shipping progress bar"

**Go to: Cart Progress** *(GROWTH plan)*

Example: "Spend $25 more for free shipping!"

1. Click **Cart Progress** in sidebar
2. Click **Create Goal**
3. Set:
   - **Goal Type**: Free Shipping
   - **Threshold**: $50 (or your amount)
   - **Message**: "Spend $X more for FREE shipping!"
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/cart-progress/README.md)

---

### ⏰ "I want urgency/countdown timers"

**Go to: Timers** *(GROWTH plan)*

Example: "Sale ends in 2:34:15"

1. Click **Timers** in sidebar
2. Click **Create Timer**
3. Set:
   - **Type**: Fixed date, Daily, or Evergreen
   - **Display**: Product page, cart, or both
   - **Style**: Choose colors/design
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/timers/README.md)

---

### 🎁 "I want free gift with purchase"

**Go to: Free Gifts** *(GROWTH plan)*

Example: "Spend $100, get a free tote bag"

1. Click **Free Gifts** in sidebar
2. Click **Create Gift**
3. Set:
   - **Trigger**: Spend amount or specific product
   - **Gift**: Select the free product
   - **Message**: "🎁 You qualify for a free gift!"
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/gifts/README.md)

---

### 👔 "I want wholesale/B2B pricing"

**Go to: Wholesale** *(GROWTH plan)*

Example: "Customers tagged 'wholesale' get 30% off"

1. Click **Wholesale** in sidebar
2. Click **Create Tier**
3. Set:
   - **Customer Tag**: "wholesale"
   - **Discount**: 30% off
   - **Products**: All or specific
4. Click **Save** → **Activate**

[Full Guide →](../features/wholesale/README.md)

---

### 🤖 "I want AI pricing recommendations"

**Go to: AI Pricing** *(PROFESSIONAL plan)*

1. Click **AI Pricing** in sidebar
2. Wait for AI to analyze your sales data (24-48 hours)
3. View recommendations
4. Apply or A/B test suggestions

[Full Guide →](../features/ai-pricing/README.md)

---

### 📊 "I want to see analytics/reports"

**Go to: Analytics** *(PROFESSIONAL plan)*

1. Click **Analytics** in sidebar
2. View:
   - Revenue from discounts
   - Most popular rules
   - Customer behavior
   - A/B test results

[Full Guide →](../features/analytics/README.md)

## Settings to Configure

Don't forget to check these settings:

### General Settings
**Settings → General**
- Currency display format
- Rounding mode
- Discount stacking behavior

### Display Settings
**Settings → Display**
- Pricing table position
- Colors and styling
- Mobile appearance

### Language
**Settings → Language**
- Choose your language (10 languages available)
- Customize text/messages

### POS (if using Shopify POS)
**Settings → POS**
- Enable/disable for in-store sales
- Configure sync options
