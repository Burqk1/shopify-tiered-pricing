## Upgrading Your Plan

### To Upgrade

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plans**
2. Click **Upgrade** on desired plan
3. Review features you'll gain
4. Click **Confirm Upgrade**
5. Approve charge in Shopify

### Prorated Billing

When upgrading mid-cycle:
- Remaining days on current plan credited
- Prorated charge for new plan
- New billing cycle starts

**Example:**
```
Current: FREE plan
Upgrading to: GROWTH ($29.99/mo)
Days remaining in cycle: 15 of 30

Credit: $0 (FREE plan)
Charge: $14.99 (15 days of GROWTH)
Next full charge: February 1 ($29.99)
```

### Immediate Access

All new features available immediately after upgrade approval.

## Downgrading Your Plan

### To Downgrade

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plans**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select lower plan
4. Review what you'll lose
5. Click **Confirm Downgrade**

### What Happens

- Access continues until current billing period ends
- Rules exceeding new plan limit are paused (not deleted)
- Features not in new plan become inaccessible
- Data is preserved

## Billing Through Shopify

### How It Works

All billing goes through Shopify:
1. Charges appear on your Shopify invoice
2. Use existing payment method
3. Managed in Shopify billing settings
4. Covered by Shopify's billing policies

### Viewing Invoices

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plans**
2. Click **View Billing History**
3. Or view in Shopify Admin > Settings > Billing

### Invoice Details

Each invoice shows:
- Billing period
- Plan name
- Amount charged
- Taxes (if applicable)
- Payment status

## Cancellation

### To Cancel

1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plans**
2. Click **Cancel Subscription**
3. Tell us why (optional)
4. Confirm cancellation

### After Cancellation

- Access continues until billing period ends
- Downgrades to FREE plan
- Rules beyond limit are paused
- Data preserved for 90 days
- Can resubscribe anytime

### Resubscribing

To resubscribe after cancellation:
1. Go to **Settings > Billing & Plans**
2. Select desired plan
3. Complete checkout
4. Paused rules reactivated

## Payment Issues

### Failed Payment

If payment fails:
1. Check payment method in Shopify
2. Ensure sufficient funds/credit
3. Update payment info if needed
4. Retry payment

### Grace Period

After failed payment:
- 3-day grace period
- App continues working
- Daily retry attempts
- Notification emails sent

### Account Suspension

If payment not resolved:
- App features disabled
- Data preserved
- Resolve payment to restore
