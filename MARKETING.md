## Support Response Templates

### Common Issue: Discount not showing

Hi {name},

Thanks for reaching out! I see the discount isn't appearing on your product pages. Let's fix that:

**Quick checklist:**

1. **Is the rule active?**
   Go to Pricing Rules and check the status is "Active" (green badge)

2. **Is the theme block added?**
   Online Store > Themes > Customize > Product page > Add block > Apps > Volume Discount Table

3. **Does the product match the rule conditions?**
   Check if the product is included in the rule (specific product, collection, or "All Products")

4. **Try a hard refresh**
   Ctrl+F5 (Windows) or Cmd+Shift+R (Mac)

Still not working? Send me:
- Your store URL
- Which product you're testing
- A screenshot of your rule settings

I'll take a look personally.

Best,
Support Team
